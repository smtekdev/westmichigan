How we work

An engagement that follows your fiscal year.

This is the actual sequence every client moves through with us, in order — not a one-off project.

Discovery & books review

We audit your current books, systems, and entity structure to find what's costing you time or money today.

Plan the fiscal year

A written tax and cash flow plan tied to your actual goals — hiring, equipment, expansion, or exit.

Run it monthly

Ongoing bookkeeping, payroll, and check-ins so decisions get made with current numbers, not last quarter's.

Close & file

Year-end close, tax filing, and a debrief on what to change before the next cycle starts.
